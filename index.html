About Sean Arnold

I am a fourth-year student studying Game Design and Development at Rochester Institute of Technology. I have been playing games my entire life and knew from an early age that I wanted to make video games. I am especially interested in how games make use of level design to teach players and have written a research paper on the subject. I thoroughly enjoy the act of creating something that will be enjoyed by others whether it be games or software in general.
