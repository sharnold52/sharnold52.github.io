About Sean Arnold

I am an RIT graduate with a B.S. in Game Design and development. After graduating in 2020, my first job in the mobile game industry was as a level tester. Eventually I moved on to become a level designer for mobile games and that is what I currently work on. In my free time, I try to learn new skills by working on passion projects in various engines like Unreal Engine 4 or Unity. I also enjoy creative writing and have one game that I self-published on steam which was my first real foray into narrative design for games. The game is called "The Peresmeshnik" and I am proud to have made it with the limited time and resources that I had. I am ready to take the next step in my game design/development journey, wherever that may be.
